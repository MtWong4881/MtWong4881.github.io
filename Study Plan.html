Assignment & Exam Countdown Study Plan

University Study Plan

Week 1: Kickoff Week (10 April – 14 April)

Goal: Familiarise yourself with the syllabus and identify weak areas.

Time Subject Activity
9:00 AM - 10:30 AM Surveying Review basics and key formulas
10:30 AM - 12:00 PM Mechanics of Materials Light review of key concepts
1:00 PM - 2:00 PM Construction Materials Theory and case studies
2:30 PM - 3:30 PM Matrix Revisit basic matrix operations

Week 2: Balanced Study Week (15 April – 21 April)

Goal: Cover the basics for all subjects while prioritising Surveying.

Time Subject Activity
9:00 AM - 10:30 AM Surveying Theory, diagrams, and practice problems
10:30 AM - 12:00 PM Mechanics of Materials Focus on problem-solving
1:00 PM - 2:00 PM Construction Materials Memorise key processes
2:30 PM - 3:30 PM Matrix Practice solving simple problems

Week 3: Practice Week (22 April – 30 April)

Goal: Solve past papers and focus on application-based learning.

  • Surveying: Mock tests and diagrams
  • Mechanics of Materials: Exam-style problems
  • Construction Materials: Theory + practical applications
  • Matrix: Practice advanced questions

Week 4: Final Prep (1 May – 22 May)

Goal: Prioritise exam-specific preparation and intensive revision.

  • Surveying: Final review before the exam (16 May)
  • Mechanics of Materials: Focus on weak areas (exam on 20 May)
  • Construction Materials: Memorise key concepts (exam on 21 May)
  • Matrix: Solve timed past papers (exam on 22 May)
